Introduction

Welcome to Calculator ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Calculator application (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Calculations and History

All calculation expressions and results are stored locally on your device using Hive (a local database). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

App Settings

Your preferences such as theme mode (light/dark) are stored locally on your device. No personal settings are sent externally.

Advertising

Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use certain information for ad personalization. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifier (Advertising ID)
  • IP address (approximate location)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

Internet Connectivity

The App checks for internet connectivity to determine whether to load advertisements. No personal data is transmitted during this check.

How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the calculator functionality
  • Store your calculation history locally
  • Display advertisements (via Google AdMob)
  • Improve the App's performance and user experience

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. The only third-party service integrated is Google AdMob for advertising purposes.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All calculation data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible by us or any third party.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is intended for general audiences and does not require user registration.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated effective date.
